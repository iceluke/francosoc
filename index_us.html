A few words about us

The UCL Francophone Society was created twelve years ago to celebrate and promote the rich history and culture of Francophone countries and it has now become one of the largest cultural societies on campus. Our community of over 250 members is made of a people from a variety of background but who all share a common passion for France!

Classes

We organise weekly classes taught by native speakers at four different levels* for students wanting to learn more about the French language and culture.

*Levels are aligned to the Common European Framework of Reference for Leanguages (CEFR).
